PR title example: Add centroid-based DOA tracking

Concise description:
Replace the slow-path speaker map logic with a simple centroid tracker driven directly by localization DOAs. Add configurable hard-window and Gaussian centroid association modes, expose the centroid match window in benchmarking, and make the new tracker the default public tracking path.

Deprecated:
`legacy`, `multi_peak_v2`, and `dominant_lock_v1` tracking modes now fail fast with a deprecated error; use `doa_centroid_v1` instead.

## Summary

Add a live directionality preview to the data collection UI and align the XVF3800 frontend DOA display to the cable reference.

## What Changed

- added a frontend-only directionality visualization to the data collection page that follows live `speaker_state` updates during capture
- updated the XVF3800 stage display so mic markers reflect the current physical labeling and channel mapping
- rotated the frontend DOA display so `0°` is shown at the cable between mics 1 and 2
- moved mic labels closer to the stage edge and simplified them to `1` through `4`, with channel mapping on hover

## Why

This makes live data collection easier to sanity-check by showing whether channel ordering and directional tracking look plausible without changing the backend capture flow.
